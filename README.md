VoiceMap : AI-Powered Brand Protection Intelligence
Track : Consumer and Retail Intelligence
Problem Stetement:Modern businesses struggle to transform massive volumes of raw, multilingual, and noisy customer review data into actionable product and marketing intelligence. Traditional sentiment analysis is often too surface-level, failing to capture specific feature-level feedback or detect emerging trends before they escalate. There is a critical need for an AI-powered Customer Review Intelligence Platform that automates the extraction of granular insights and prioritizes recommendations through a non-technical interface, bridging the gap between raw feedback and strategic decision-making.
Solution:
-Multi-Source & Language Support: It collects reviews from Amazon, Flipkart, YouTube, and Twitter in English, Hindi, and Kannada, making sure no customer feedback is missed.

-AI Data Cleaning: The system automatically flags bot reviews, removes spam, and detects sarcasm, ensuring the brand only sees genuine complaints.

-Specific Feature Analysis: Instead of general summaries, it tells you exactly what is wrong—like battery, packaging, or sound—and how confident the AI is about that finding.

-The Danger Score (0–100): It ranks every problem by how fast it is growing and its potential to go viral, helping teams prioritize the biggest risks.

-Revenue at Risk: It translates abstract scores into real money, showing exactly how many rupees the brand might lose this month if the issue isn't fixed.

-Instant WhatsApp Alerts: Managers get immediate notifications on their phones for critical "ACT NOW" problems so they can respond in real-time.

-Voice-to-Text Transcription: It automatically transcribes audio and video reviews (like YouTube unboxings) so the brand can analyze what people are saying as easily as what they are writing.

-Customer Recovery Dashboard: Tracks high-risk customer complaints from detected, In Progress & Resolved, ensuring teams respond quickly and prevent customer loss.

-Recovery Insights: Uses sentiment analysis to suggest actions (apology, support contact, replacement) and monitors if customer sentiment improves after resolution.
